Common Philadelphia home challenges we handle

Freeze-Thaw Damage

Water freezes in masonry joints, expands, and pulls brick apart. Same with concrete steps and walkways. We inspect mortar joints in fall, re-point as needed, and pre-winterize hose bibs and exposed pipes.

Old Plumbing & Cast Iron

Many Philly homes still have cast-iron drain stacks and galvanized supply lines decades past their life. We monitor for slow drains, low pressure, and rust spots — and replace strategically rather than all-at-once.

Row House Specifics

Shared walls, party-wall flashing, flat or near-flat roofs, third-floor 'pilot houses' — the structural quirks of a Philly row home need a team that knows them. We do.

Heating System Reliability

Old hot-water radiators, single-pipe steam, oil-to-gas conversions — Philly heating systems are rarely simple. We bleed radiators, check pressure, and pre-season every fall so you're not calling at 2am in January.
