Privacy Policy

How Handyman Gold Hands LLC collects, uses, and protects your information.

Effective Date: April 25, 2026

Welcome to Handyman Gold Hands LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data when you visit mybotbestagent-maker.github.io/gold-hands-membership/ or handymangoldhands.com (collectively, the "Site") or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and information gathered automatically when you use our Site.

Information you provide:

  • Name — when you fill out a contact or membership sign-up form.
  • Email address — for account creation, billing receipts, and service communications.
  • Phone number — to schedule and confirm service appointments.
  • Service address — to verify you are within our service area and dispatch technicians.
  • Payment information — credit/debit card details processed securely by Stripe (we never store raw card numbers).
  • Service notes & messages — any details you enter in form fields about requested work.

Information collected automatically:

  • Browser type, device, operating system, and IP address.
  • Pages visited, time spent, and referring URLs via Google Analytics.
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies (see Section 6).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery — scheduling appointments, dispatching handymen, and completing work orders.
  • Billing & payments — processing recurring membership charges and one-time service fees through Stripe.
  • Account management — creating and maintaining your membership account and service history.
  • Communications — sending appointment confirmations, receipts, service updates, and important account notices.
  • Marketing (opt-in only) — sending promotional emails about new services or offers if you have opted in. You may unsubscribe at any time.
  • Site improvement — analyzing usage patterns via Google Analytics to improve our website and user experience.
  • Legal compliance — meeting our legal obligations and resolving disputes.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with the following trusted service providers solely to operate our business:

  • Stripe, Inc. — payment processing. Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy.
  • Formspree — form submission handling. Formspree's privacy policy is at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.
  • Google Analytics — website analytics. Data is aggregated and anonymized where possible. You may opt out via Google's opt-out tool.
  • Field technicians — your name, phone, and service address are shared with our vetted handymen only to fulfill your booked appointment.
  • Legal authorities — we may disclose information when required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience on our Site. Cookies we use include:

  • Essential cookies — required for the Site to function (e.g., session management).
  • Analytics cookies — Google Analytics cookies to understand traffic and behavior patterns.
  • Preference cookies — remember your settings and choices.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some Site functionality.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your membership or service relationship is active, plus a reasonable period thereafter for legal, billing, and dispute resolution purposes:

  • Account & billing records — retained for 7 years to comply with financial and tax regulations.
  • Service history — retained for 3 years after your last service.
  • Marketing preferences — retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion.
  • Analytics data — retained per Google Analytics default settings (26 months).

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — request that inaccurate or incomplete data be corrected.
  • Deletion — request that we delete your personal data (subject to legal retention obligations).
  • Opt-out of marketing — unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time via the link in any email we send.
  • Data portability — request a machine-readable copy of your data where technically feasible.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the details below. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, access controls, and secure third-party payment processing via Stripe. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and to contact us immediately if you suspect any unauthorized access to your account.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Site and services are intended for adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of our Site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
